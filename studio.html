How to publish

  1. Click Download .md and save it into blog/posts/.
  2. Put any images into blog/images/ (use the exact filenames you referenced).
  3. Click Copy index entry and paste it at the top of the array in blog/posts.js.
  4. Commit & push. The post appears automatically.

Tip: reference images in the body as [IMAGE: images/name.jpg]: the renderer maps that to blog/images/ for you.
