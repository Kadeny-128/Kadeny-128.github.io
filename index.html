Living & learning

Kaden Yeung

Student, builder, and writer working at the intersection of AI, science, and human curiosity.

Between 15 and 17, I interned at Singularity Health, an AI healthcare startup, and in the e-business department of UOB Kay Hian, an Asian investment bank.

Last year I built PAC-Dose, a research project tackling desalination-plant barriers through an automatic coagulant-dosing system. I learned a ton about desalination, and the whole thing was an exciting sprint.

Earlier this year I coded Consensus, a dashboard that leverages Cerebras for massively parallel agent execution in drug discovery.

Outside of work, I read a lot about astrophysics and philosophy. I share the occasional thought on Substack and am planning to launch a podcast soon. But after all — what do I really know?
